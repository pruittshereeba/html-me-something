I love to travel!!!



    Places I've Traveled to:

  • Cozumel, Mexico
  • Cayman Islands
  • Nassau, Bahamas
  • Biloxi, MS

    • Cozumel

    Cozumel turned out to be my favorite island! Although it was the hottest, it was the most enjoyable. My friends and I found great souvenirs there, swam with the fish, and sampled some local goodies while our ship was docked there. The view was breath-taking with well maintained beaches and lots to do! And... believe it or not, I got the most golden tan there, my skin was glowy golden for at least a week after!

    Cayman

    Cayman was a lot of fun as well, we spent a day there and had a cookout. We chilled in the sun and and watched the waves and bought cool souvenirs. There was a flea market shop there that had hair braiders, a friend of mine had her hair braided and enjoyed learning about the braiders' family histories!

    Nassau

    Nassau, Nassau, Nassau!!!! If you've never been I'd recommend it! We visited Atlantis, took a bus tour of the island, and sampled local food there. We went to a history museum that outlined Nassau's rich tourism history, and hung out with other cruisers in a nightclub. We found out that one thing that the locals do to make money is to be lookalikes, of celebrities like Ludacris! The lookalike didn't really look like Ludacris, but I gave him an A for effort! I had a great time at Senor Frogs, nice bar, great music, and great drinks. I was right at the port, so we could make sure not to get left on the island!!!

    Biloxi, MS

    In Biloxi, Mississippi I attended a Pruitt Family Reunion. My family is from a small town called Macon, Mississippi; so we traveled to Biloxi for the reunion. We stayed at the Treasure Bay Casino Resort Hotel and did lots of activities, like a family wellness day on the beach, gambled in the Casino, and had a black tie formal banquet. My family is an awesome group of people and being able to spend 3 days with them in such a beautiful place was a lot of fun and helped us to unify even more! The staff was hospitable and very good at managing such a large group of people for the event.
